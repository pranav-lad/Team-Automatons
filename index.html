About Us

Team Automatons (PCCOE)

Robocon is an inter-national level robotics competition held in various Asian countries organized by Asia Broadcasting Union (ABU). In India the event is organized by ‘Doordarshan’. Each year in the month of August a theme is proposed by one of the country for the contest. The statement is very challenging and give student brains a real engineering exercise. Each year two robots are manufactured, Autonomous and Manual.
